You can manage your account, including your personal information, password and account deletion.

## Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account, you can do so by following these steps:

1. **Log in to your account**: Go to the [FreeIPAPI](https://freeipapi.com/login) and log in with your credentials.
2. **Navigate to the Account section**: Once logged in, go to the "Profile Picture" section in your account dashboard and click on "Account".
3. **Delete your account**: Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the "Delete Account" button. You will be prompted to confirm your decision.
4. **Confirm deletion**: After confirming, your account will be deleted, and you will no longer have access to your data or the API.

<Note>
    By deleting your account, if you have an active subscription, it will be canceled immediately, and you will not be charged for the next billing cycle.
</Note>
