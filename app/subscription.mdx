FreeIPAPI offers a subscription service that allows you to access the API without any limitations.

## Subscription Plans

After you sign up, you can navigate to the [Subscription](https://freeipapi.com/subscription) page to choose a plan that suits your needs.

## Payment Methods

We offer the following payment methods:

- **Credit Card**: You can pay using your credit card through our secure payment gateway.
- **PayPal**: If you prefer using PayPal, you can select this option during checkout.

## Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Once you cancel, you will not be charged for the next billing cycle, but you will retain access to the API until the end of your current billing period.

## Contact Support

If you have any questions or need assistance with your subscription, please contact our support team at [support@freeipapi.com](mailto:support@freeipapi.com).
